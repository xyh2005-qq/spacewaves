About Space Waves

Space Waves is an exciting arcade game where you control an arrow through 33 challenging levels. Navigate through obstacles, avoid walls, rotating cogs, and thorns to reach the end of each level. With varying difficulty levels, you can choose your own adventure!

33 Unique Levels

Each level offers different challenges and difficulty ratings

Multiple Platforms

Play on desktop or mobile devices through your browser

Free to Play

No downloads required - play instantly in your browser

How to Play

Desktop Controls

- Hold left mouse button to go up
- Release to go down
- Press P to pause

Mobile Controls

- Hold screen to go up
- Release to go down

Pro Tips

Move in zigzag patterns when facing obstacles to maintain momentum and control
